HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED

Professional Tanzanian construction and civil engineering specialists delivering high-quality works across bridges, roads, maintenance and more.

OUR STORY

HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED was founded in 2021 and is a private company formed and managed by professional and well qualified Tanzanians dealing in construction works.

The company is engaged in engineering in the construction industry, specializing in civil engineering works. Leveraging the technical expertise and management experience of the key company personnel, HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED is positioned to deliver high quality services.

We have delivered work for several clients across a wide range of construction activities including bridge and culvert construction, routine, periodic and recurrent maintenance works, bridge preventive works, tower erections and access roads.

MISSION / VISION

Vision

HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED aims at becoming an effective and competitive partner in fostering services in the construction industry in Tanzania and providing world class services to our clients.

Mission

HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED is dedicated to adding value to our esteemed clients’ project development funds through provision of quality services at competitive terms with safety, health and environment as top priority. This is achieved by adopting and adhering to the highest engineering & business ethics/standards coupled with employing highly qualified and innovative staff.

COMPANY POLICY

HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED recognizes that every client is entitled to receive the best product for their project in terms of engineering and environmental performance, and that it should be safe and without risk to health. To this end, every reasonable effort will be made and positive action taken to secure the health, safety and welfare of all personnel during project implementation and the usage of the end product.

MANAGEMENT & PERSONNEL

HADA INVESTMENT COMPANY LIMITED aims to optimize business performance by employing competent and committed people who work to meet standards of design and clients' expectations. The consultancy is managed by highly qualified and dedicated personnel assisted by experts and associates in general engineering, environmental management and project management.
