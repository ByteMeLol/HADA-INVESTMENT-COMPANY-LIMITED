Education-(What we deliver)

Our education projects go beyond buildings. We deliver safe, durable and flexible learning environments that support modern pedagogy and community use.

  • Scope: Classrooms, lecture halls, dormitories, staff housing, sanitation, utilities and siteworks.
  • Deliverables: Detailed design drawings, BOQ, phased construction programmes, QA/QC reports and handover manuals.
  • Standards & Safety: Designs comply with local building codes and include child-safe site planning and MEP coordination.
  • Examples: Upgrades to classroom blocks, new hostel construction, and campus drainage improvements.

Health-(Clinical & compliant delivery)

Healthcare projects require strict infection control, correct zoning and reliable services. We manage construction so facilities are safe, efficient and maintainable.

  • Scope: Wards, operating theatres, laboratories, pharmacies, medical gas, and utility services.
  • Deliverables: Clinical layouts, materials schedules, commissioning protocols, and staff training for handover.
  • Compliance: We coordinate with health authorities and follow international best-practice for infection control and accessibility.
  • Approach: Phased construction to keep clinical services operational where required; prefabrication where appropriate to reduce on-site time.

Residence-(Homes and hospitality)

Residential and hospitality projects focus on user experience, longevity and buildability from efficient apartment layouts to high-quality finishes for hotels.

  • Scope: Multi-unit apartments, single-family homes, hotels, internal fit-out and MEP systems.
  • Deliverables: Design development, interfaces with services, FF&E coordination and snagging lists for practical completion.
  • Quality & Warranty: Durable materials selection and clear maintenance handover documentation to reduce lifecycle costs.
  • Value Engineering: We offer alternatives that preserve quality while optimising cost and construction time.

Commercial-(Retail & event spaces)

Commercial projects demand careful coordination of services, circulation and life-safety systems to ensure efficient operations and pleasant customer experiences.

  • Scope: Market halls, retail fit-out, food courts, access, parking and building services coordination.
  • Deliverables: Phasing plans, service coordination drawings, on-site logistics and post-handover support.
  • Operations-focused design: Durable finishes, easy maintenance routes and effective pedestrian/vehicular flow.

Transport-(Roads, bridges, access works)

We deliver transport infrastructure from earthworks and drainage to pavement construction and structural bridges, focusing on durability and maintainability.

  • Scope: Road construction, pavement design, culverts, bridges, shoulder works and traffic management during construction.
  • Deliverables: Geotechnical review, pavement design, QA sampling and testing, and maintenance plans.
  • Durability: Material selection and drainage design to extend pavement life and reduce long-term costs.

Industrial-(Workshops, warehouses & fabrication)

Industrial facilities require robust structural design, efficient layouts for workflows and reliable services for heavy-duty operations.

  • Scope: Workshop buildings, warehouse design, heavy-duty floors, crane supports and utility distribution.
  • Deliverables: Structural and service coordination, sequencing for fit-out and commissioning checklists.
  • Safety & Compliance: Fire safety strategy, hazardous materials handling zones and access controls.
